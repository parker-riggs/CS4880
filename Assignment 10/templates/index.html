How to Play

W A S D
or ←↑↓→
Move your character through the world
E
Interact — talk to NPCs, read signs, use stairs
Tab
Open your inventory & character sheet
Q
Open the quest journal
Esc
Close dialogue / sign, or open the pause menu
⚔ In Battle
W / S
or ↑↓
Navigate the battle menu (Fight / Item / Flee)
Space
or Enter
Confirm selection — and strike during the timing bar
◆ Tip Walk up to an NPC or sign and a ! will appear above them. In battle, hit Space when the cursor is over the green zone for a critical strike.
